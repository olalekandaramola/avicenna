Our Activities

Education is far beyond the four wall of the classroom
that why we engage our students in activities that improves them physically and intellectually.

  • Music
  • Learn
  • Painting
  • Sports
  • Playground

Music

Music introduces children to the sounds and meanings of a different of words and helps strengthen thier memory skills. We provide collection of nursery rhymes and poetry to our pupils, which enables them to learn easily and also aid thier retentiveness.

Music or song recitation enables children to learn faster and also serve as a means of fun. At Avicenna we are aware of all benefits attached to learning music to children and that why we have created time for music. Benefits of music to children are :

  •   Strenghten memory
  •   Ease learning
  •   Intellectual growth
  •   Reduces stress
  •   Builds confidence
  •   Fun

Learn

Learning is the acquisition of knowledge or skills through study, experience, or being taught. Learning can absolutely be done anywhere, but learning in schoool(formal education) still remains one of the best form of education that can be given to a child.

At Avicenna Elementary School the education we give to our pupils is beyond ability to read and write. We instill discipline, make them understand the value of hardwork and perseverance, build good character... other benefits includes:

  •   Quality education
  •   Discipline
  •   Good moral
  •   valuable knowledge
  •   Active Learning
  •   Increase confidence

Painting

Children generally loves art (painting, drawing...). Painting helps the creative development in kids and the ability to be creative is vital to the success of our children in today's world. Painting also makes the learning process fun and easy for kids.

Most times, we can observe children smiling as they paint, draw and color. This shows that painting is a source of happiness to them.We recongnise the powerful influence painting and art as whole can have on children. Benefits of painting to kids are:

  •   Unlocks creativity
  •   Builds self-esteem
  •   Fun
  •   Communication of emotions
  •   Simplify learning process
  •   Intellectual growth

Sports

Allowing children to get involve in extracurricular activities such as sports, helps them explore and develop skills which might not have been dicovered without it. Participating in sports (football, running...) enhance physical and mental development of children.

At Avicenna Elementary School we provide our pupils the chance to perticipate in various sports that can improve them physically and mentally like playing football, running, excercise and lot more. Benefits of sport to children:

  •   Improves self-esteem
  •   Instill discipline
  •   Have fun
  •   Physical growth
  •   Improves team spirit
  •   Intellectual growth

Playground

While school is primarily a learning environment, playgrounds within school brings alot of benefits to children.At Avicenna Elementary School we understand that play is critical for kids to develop the emotional, social and creative thinking skills they will be needing.

Children look forward to playtime because it gives them chance to see thier friends and engage activities that improves them physically and mentally. Some benefits of playground(play) within school to children:

  •   Improves Relationship
  •   Physical growth
  •   Intellectual growth
  •   Self discovery
  •   Builds confidence
  •   Improves Communication
